    TIME IN AMAN VERSUS IN ARDA

    Suilad! In the world of Middle Earth and the divine realm of Aman (Valinor) where the Valars dwell, time unfolds uniquely through two distinct measurements: the Valiant Year and the Sun Year. The Valiant Year symbolizes the passage of time within the blessed lands, where 1 Valiant Year equates to a staggering 144 Sun Years. Conversely, the Sun Year, prevalent in Middle Earth, aligns 1 Valiant Year to 10 Sun Years. To effortlessly navigate the time converter tool, select either the Valiant Year or Sun Year. Should the Valiant Year be your choice, expect 1 Valiant Year to convert to 144 Sun Years. On the contrary, opting for the Sun Year translates 1 Valiant Year into 10 Sun Years, enabling a seamless transition between these distinctive temporal realms.
