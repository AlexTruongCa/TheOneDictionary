The Valiant Year symbolizes the passage of time within the blessed lands, where 1 Valiant Year equates to a staggering 144 Sun Years. Conversely, the Sun Year, prevalent in Middle Earth, aligns 1 Valiant Year to 10 Sun Years. To effortlessly navigate the time converter tool, select either the Valiant Year or Sun Year. On the contrary, opting for the Sun Year translates 1 Valiant Year into 10 Sun Years, enabling a seamless transition between these distinctive temporal realms.
